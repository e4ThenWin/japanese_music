Japanese music suggestions

Goal

The goal of this website is to provide Japanese music recommendations based on the kanji (漢字) that you know. This will be achieved in the following manner...

1. Record known kanji

First I need to get a list of known kanji from the user. There are several different (and progressively harder) ways to achieve this.

  1. Have them enter in known kanji
  2. Read from a file
  3. Use Wanikani API

2. Keep a list of songs with all of the lyrics

I'll need to have a massive database that keeps tracks of songs as well as the kanji used inside of them. To keep it simple I will only keep track of the jouyou kanji(常用漢字) which means that I need to keep track of 2136 characters. I will also implement this one step at a time such as only starting off with one song and 1 kanji character (ex: person --> 人)

3. Recommend songs based on number of known kanji entered

For this final step I will compare the array of known kanji from the user with my massive library of songs and return songs that have the highest matched percentage of known kanji. Below I've wrote out a small example of what I would like to achieve.

EXAMPLE

Known Kanji: [言全]

Song: Yoru Ni Kakeru (夜に駆ける)

Lyrics: 「さよなら」だけだった その一言で全てが分かった

Percent matched: 66.7%
